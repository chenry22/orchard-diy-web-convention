The convention

The first ever Orchard DIY Web Convention will take place on April 11, 2026 from 11am-1pm. If you're in the St. Louis area, reach out to @reparativecomputing on Instagram for the location!

The convention will consist of a brief introduction and maybe a presentation (if we decide to do one), after which everyone will be released to create their own handmade website. Part of this will include creating a webring live and in-person. Anyone who wants to will be able to present and share their site, and they will be collected and linked to on this page!
